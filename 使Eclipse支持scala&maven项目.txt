使Eclipse支持Scala的Maven工程:
按照上述过程完成第一步的版本加载过程后,接下来让eclipse能够新建scala的maven工程.
同第一步安装插件一样,链接:http://alchim31.free.fr/m2e-scala/update-site/此URL是安装Maven for Eclipse.
(一共需要下载15步,如果一遍不能安装成功,不要泄气,多下载并安装几次.本人使用了vpn安装了3次才完成)
可见附件1

在maven工程中,搜索"scala-arch",如果不能找到scala项目,则在Window->Preferences->Maven->Archetypes增加一个Remote Catalog
url:http://repo.maven.apache.org/maven2/archetype-catalog.xml
description:Remote Catalog

pom.xml文件内容如下:
<project xmlns="http://maven.apache.org/POM/4.0.0" xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance" xsi:schemaLocation="http://maven.apache.org/POM/4.0.0 http://maven.apache.org/maven-v4_0_0.xsd">
  <modelVersion>4.0.0</modelVersion>
  <groupId>com.soargift</groupId>
  <artifactId>soar</artifactId>
  <version>0.0.1-SNAPSHOT</version>
  <name>${project.artifactId}</name>
  <description>My wonderfull scala app</description>
  <inceptionYear>2015</inceptionYear>
  <licenses>
    <license>
      <name>My License</name>
      <url>http://....</url>
      <distribution>repo</distribution>
    </license>
  </licenses>

  <properties>
    <maven.compiler.source>1.6</maven.compiler.source>
    <maven.compiler.target>1.6</maven.compiler.target>
    <encoding>UTF-8</encoding>
    <scala.version>2.11.5</scala.version>
    <scala.compat.version>2.11</scala.compat.version>
  </properties>

  <dependencies>
    <dependency>
      <groupId>org.scala-lang</groupId>
      <artifactId>scala-library</artifactId>
      <version>${scala.version}</version>
    </dependency>

    <!-- Test -->
    <dependency>
      <groupId>junit</groupId>
      <artifactId>junit</artifactId>
      <version>4.11</version>
      <scope>test</scope>
    </dependency>
    <dependency>
      <groupId>org.specs2</groupId>
      <artifactId>specs2-core_${scala.compat.version}</artifactId>
      <version>2.4.16</version>
      <scope>test</scope>
    </dependency>

    <!---增加此配置为了解决Junit的错误-->
    <dependency>
        <groupId>org.specs2</groupId>
        <artifactId>specs2-junit_${scala.compat.version}</artifactId>
        <version>2.4.16</version>
        <scope>test</scope>
    </dependency>
    <dependency>
      <groupId>org.scalatest</groupId>
      <artifactId>scalatest_${scala.compat.version}</artifactId>
      <version>2.2.4</version>
      <scope>test</scope>
    </dependency>
  </dependencies>

  <build>
    <sourceDirectory>src/main/scala</sourceDirectory>
    <testSourceDirectory>src/test/scala</testSourceDirectory>
    <plugins>
      <plugin>
        <!-- see http://davidb.github.com/scala-maven-plugin -->
        <groupId>net.alchim31.maven</groupId>
        <artifactId>scala-maven-plugin</artifactId>
        <version>3.2.0</version>
        <executions>
          <execution>
            <goals>
              <goal>compile</goal>
              <goal>testCompile</goal>
            </goals>
            <configuration>
              <args>
                <arg>-make:transitive</arg>
                <arg>-dependencyfile</arg>
                <arg>${project.build.directory}/.scala_dependencies</arg>
              </args>
            </configuration>
          </execution>
        </executions>
      </plugin>
      <plugin>
        <groupId>org.apache.maven.plugins</groupId>
        <artifactId>maven-surefire-plugin</artifactId>
        <version>2.18.1</version>
        <configuration>
          <useFile>false</useFile>
          <disableXmlReport>true</disableXmlReport>
          <!-- If you have classpath issue like NoDefClassError,... -->
          <!-- useManifestOnlyJar>false</useManifestOnlyJar -->
          <includes>
            <include>**/*Test.*</include>
            <include>**/*Suite.*</include>
          </includes>
        </configuration>
      </plugin>
    </plugins>
  </build>
</project>

默认生成的pom.xml有bug,增加对JUnit的支持即可.
(创建maven项目,本人操作了3次,前两次都报错,"error while loading Suite, Scala signature Suite has wrong version expected: 5.0 found: 4.1 in Suite.class")
此错误出现的原因被我验证出来了,当引入了scala和spark,hadoop相关包,如果引入org.specs包就会报错.
(猜测错误原因:本人使用的STS的版本相当于Eclipse的4.5.1,并且maven版本可能也比较高级了,网上有一些配置内容的版本比较低,要求引入org.specs,如下:)
错误示例:
    <dependency>
      <groupId>org.specs</groupId>
      <artifactId>specs</artifactId>
      <version>1.2.5</version>
      <scope>test</scope>
    </dependency>
所以,与如下的内容有了冲突,所以,省略配置org.specs的内容:
    <dependency>
			<groupId>org.specs2</groupId>
			<artifactId>specs2-core_${scala.compat.version}</artifactId>
			<version>2.4.16</version>
			<scope>test</scope>
		</dependency>
		<dependency>
			<groupId>org.specs2</groupId>
			<artifactId>specs2-junit_${scala.compat.version}</artifactId>
			<version>2.4.16</version>
			<scope>test</scope>
		</dependency>

<project xmlns="http://maven.apache.org/POM/4.0.0" xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance"
	xsi:schemaLocation="http://maven.apache.org/POM/4.0.0 http://maven.apache.org/maven-v4_0_0.xsd">
	<modelVersion>4.0.0</modelVersion>
	<groupId>com.soargift</groupId>
	<artifactId>soar</artifactId>
	<version>0.0.1-SNAPSHOT</version>
	<name>${project.artifactId}</name>
	<description>My wonderfull scala app</description>
	<inceptionYear>2015</inceptionYear>
	<licenses>
		<license>
			<name>My License</name>
			<url>http://....</url>
			<distribution>repo</distribution>
		</license>
	</licenses>

	<properties>
		<maven.compiler.source>1.6</maven.compiler.source>
		<maven.compiler.target>1.6</maven.compiler.target>
		<encoding>UTF-8</encoding>
		<scala.version>2.11.8</scala.version>
		<scala.compat.version>2.11</scala.compat.version>
	</properties>

	<repositories>
		<repository>
			<id>scala-tools.org</id>
			<name>Scala-Tools Maven2 Repository</name>
			<url>http://scala-tools.org/repo-releases</url>
		</repository>
	</repositories>

	<pluginRepositories>
		<pluginRepository>
			<id>scala-tools.org</id>
			<name>Scala-Tools Maven2 Repository</name>
			<url>http://scala-tools.org/repo-releases</url>
		</pluginRepository>
	</pluginRepositories>


	<dependencies>
		<dependency>
			<groupId>org.scala-lang</groupId>
			<artifactId>scala-library</artifactId>
			<version>${scala.version}</version>
		</dependency>

		<!-- 增加对spark的支持 -->
		<dependency>
			<groupId>org.apache.spark</groupId>
			<artifactId>spark-core_2.11</artifactId>
			<version>1.2.1</version>
		</dependency>
		<!-- 增加对hadoop的支持 -->
		<dependency>
			<groupId>org.apache.hadoop</groupId>
			<artifactId>hadoop-client</artifactId>
			<version>2.6.0</version>
		</dependency>
		<!-- Test -->
		<dependency>
			<groupId>junit</groupId>
			<artifactId>junit</artifactId>
			<version>4.11</version>
			<scope>test</scope>
		</dependency>
		<dependency>
			<groupId>org.specs2</groupId>
			<artifactId>specs2-core_${scala.compat.version}</artifactId>
			<version>2.4.16</version>
			<scope>test</scope>
		</dependency>
		<dependency>
			<groupId>org.specs2</groupId>
			<artifactId>specs2-junit_${scala.compat.version}</artifactId>
			<version>2.4.16</version>
			<scope>test</scope>
		</dependency>
		<dependency>
			<groupId>org.scalatest</groupId>
			<artifactId>scalatest_${scala.compat.version}</artifactId>
			<version>2.2.6</version>
			<scope>test</scope>
		</dependency>
	</dependencies>

	<build>
		<sourceDirectory>src/main/scala</sourceDirectory>
		<testSourceDirectory>src/test/scala</testSourceDirectory>
		<plugins>
			<plugin>
				<!-- see http://davidb.github.com/scala-maven-plugin -->
				<groupId>net.alchim31.maven</groupId>
				<artifactId>scala-maven-plugin</artifactId>
				<version>3.2.0</version>
				<executions>
					<execution>
						<goals>
							<goal>compile</goal>
							<goal>testCompile</goal>
						</goals>
						<configuration>
							<args>
								<arg>-make:transitive</arg>
								<arg>-dependencyfile</arg>
								<arg>${project.build.directory}/.scala_dependencies</arg>
							</args>
						</configuration>
					</execution>
				</executions>
			</plugin>
			<plugin>
				<groupId>org.apache.maven.plugins</groupId>
				<artifactId>maven-surefire-plugin</artifactId>
				<version>2.18.1</version>
				<configuration>
					<useFile>false</useFile>
					<disableXmlReport>true</disableXmlReport>
					<!-- If you have classpath issue like NoDefClassError,... -->
					<!-- useManifestOnlyJar>false</useManifestOnlyJar -->
					<includes>
						<include>**/*Test.*</include>
						<include>**/*Suite.*</include>
					</includes>
				</configuration>
			</plugin>
			<!-- 构建jar包 -->
			<plugin>
				<artifactId>maven-assembly-plugin</artifactId>
				<version>2.6</version>
				<configuration>
					<descriptorRefs>
						<descriptorRef>jar-with-dependencies</descriptorRef>
					</descriptorRefs>
				</configuration>
			</plugin>

		</plugins>
	</build>
</project>
